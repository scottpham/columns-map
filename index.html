Aerial Structures in The Bay Area

Many of the piers that hold up aerial structures (bridges, on-ramps, overpasses, etc.) have to be retrofitted for earthquake safety, especially along the BART lines. A lot of that work has been finished already, but much of it won't be done till 2019.

Notes: There's a lot of local bridges that have been fixed, too.
